The short version: Shukr Daily does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server. All your data — your counts, journal entries, reminders, and preferences — stays on your device. We have no servers, no databases, and no way to access your information.

1Overview

Shukr Daily is an Islamic gratitude habit-building app designed to help Muslims cultivate daily gratitude through dhikr, journaling, and reminders. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2Data Collection

Shukr Daily does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server. All your data stays on your device.

Data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Gratitude counter (daily and lifetime counts)
  • Gratitude journal entries
  • Reminder settings and schedules
  • App preferences (theme, notification settings)
  • Streak information and statistics

100% Offline

This data is never uploaded, shared, or accessible to anyone other than you. We have no servers, no databases, and no way to access your information.

3User Accounts

Shukr Daily does not require or support user accounts. There is no login, no registration, and no email collection. You can use the app completely anonymously.

4Advertising

Shukr Daily displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob to support the free app. Google AdMob may collect certain data for ad personalization and analytics, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Device information (model, OS version)

This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Opt out of personalized ads

Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

5Permissions

Shukr Daily requests the following permissions — each tied to a specific feature, nothing more:

Notifications
To deliver your scheduled Shukar and Sadaqah reminders at the times you choose.
Internet
Required only for loading advertisements.
Vibration
For haptic feedback when using the counter.
Boot Completed
To reschedule your reminders after device restart.
Exact Alarm
To deliver reminders at precise times.
Wake Lock
To ensure reminders fire even when the device is sleeping.

6Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secure with a strong password or biometric lock.

Important

If you uninstall the app or clear app data, all your information will be permanently deleted. We cannot recover this data as we do not have access to it.

7Data Backup

Your data may be included in Android's automatic backup system if you have enabled it in your device settings. This backup is encrypted and managed by Google, not by us.

8Children's Privacy

Shukr Daily does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app contains no content inappropriate for any age group and is suitable for the entire family.

9Third-Party Services

Shukr Daily uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements (see Section 4)

We do not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or other third-party tracking.

10Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access — All your data is accessible within the app
  • Delete — You can delete individual entries or reset all data in Settings
  • Export — Future updates may include data export functionality

12Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how Shukr Daily handles your data, please reach out.
